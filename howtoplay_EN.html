Triple Vision Mono (Version 5.5)

How to play

Ride your music with 3 people. Collect the blocks matching the colour of your ship/thrusters. Continue to collect your own colour blocks to keep the combo going!

Controls

Position Gamepad Control Keyboard Control Mouse Control Blocks to Collect
Left Player 2 Z and X No Normal
Centre Player 1* Arrow Keys* No Inverted
Right Player 3 , and . Yes Normal
*Press B (button or key) to toggle single player controls
